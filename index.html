Introduction

I'm an aspiring full stack developer currently enrolled in Rutgers' Coding Bootcamp. I graduated from Rutgers with a double major in IT and Psychology in 2015. I've had a passion for web design and development since being introduced to it in an Intro to Computer Science class in high school. I worked as a Web Coordinator at Porcelanosa, a luxury European kitchen, tile and bath firm, for three years. I was part of the marketing department and worked on national campaigns along with updating and maintaining the corporate website. During my time there, I had the chance to work directly with developers who helped make major updates to the corporate site - including making the website ADA compliant, streamlining the mass import of hundreds of products, and updating the WordPress/Magento platforms to the latest versions. I realized that instead of managing projects, I wanted to be a developer myself.
